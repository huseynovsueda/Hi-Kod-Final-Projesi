A new scientific study reveals the true colors of Neptune and Uranus

Pallab Ghosh Title,Science correspondent, BBC News January 6, 2024
A study conducted by British astronomers has revealed that our ideas about the colors of the planets Neptune and Uranus are wrong. Images from a space mission in the 1980s showed Neptune as deep blue and Uranus as green. But a study has discovered that both planets are similar shades of greenish blue. It turns out that earlier images of Neptune were enhanced to show details of the planet's atmosphere, which changed its true color.

Capsule carrying asteroid fragments lands on Earth: May offer clues to the beginning of life

September 23, 2023 Update September 25, 2023 Jonathan Amos | BBC Science Correspondent Jonathan Amos | BBC Science Correspondent
NASA's Osiris-Rex capsule entered Earth's atmosphere 15 times faster than a bullet on Sunday with dust samples from an asteroid and landed safely. The capsule, the size of a car tire, created a fireball in the sky at 12 kilometers per second. The capsule's speed was slowed by a heat shield and parachutes, and it made a soft landing in the Western Desert in the US state of Utah. The samples, weighing about 250 grams, were taken to the Johnson Space Center in Texas for analysis.
